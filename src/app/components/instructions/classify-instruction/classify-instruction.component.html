Evaluating statements

After reading the statements carefully, you should DRAG and DROP the cards to split them up into 3 piles:
1. A pile for statements you tend to disagree with;
2. A pile for cards you tend to agree with;
3. A pile for the neutrals.
Click on the move icon to move the statements between piles

Order and quantity

The order per pile or the number of statements per pile does not matter
