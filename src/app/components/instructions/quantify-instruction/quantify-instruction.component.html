Quantify

Quantifying statements

You have to quantify each statement according to your agreement or disagreement degree

For example, you can quantify a statement as -3 for a sentence you disagree the most or 3 you agree the most.

Order and quantity

The order you quantify does not matter however as you can see at the top, each number classification has a limit

Finishing

When you quantify all statements, you can click on Continue or click Back to see the last step
